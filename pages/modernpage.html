Rizal’s Era Activism vs. Modern Activism

Rizal’s Era

  • Written Propaganda → Rizal used literature to peacefully expose injustice.
  • Reformist Approach → Focus on institutional and legal reforms under colonial rule.
  • Limited Technology → Ideas spread slowly through print and word of mouth.
  • National Identity Formation → His works solidified early Filipino consciousness.

Modern Activism

  • Digital Mobilization → Movements spread instantly through social media.
  • Youth-Led Movements → Students and young professionals lead national conversations.
  • Grassroots Advocacy → Community-centered activism aiming at local empowerment.
  • Global Awareness → Modern activists connect local issues to global struggles.

Though their forms differ, both eras are driven by the same spirit: love of country and the courage to challenge injustice.
